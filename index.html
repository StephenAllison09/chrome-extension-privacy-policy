Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2025

Persistent Highlighter & Saver is a Chrome extension designed to help users highlight and save text on any webpage. We value your privacy and want to be transparent about how we handle your data.

Data Collection and Usage

Our extension does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All highlighted text is saved locally on your device using Chrome's secure Storage API.

What Data Is Stored

The extension stores the text you choose to highlight, linked to the specific webpage where you created the highlight. This data remains within your browser and is not transmitted to any servers.

Data Removal

You can delete all stored highlights for any page using the "Clear All Highlights" button in the extension popup. Once cleared, this data cannot be recovered.

Security

All data is stored securely using Chrome's storage system. No third-party services are involved in storing or processing your data.
